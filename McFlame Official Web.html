About the server

MC Flame is a competitive Minecraft server built for players who want more than just survival. With custom rank systems, unique gameplay modes, and an active community, every session is a new adventure.

Whether you're a seasoned veteran or brand new to Minecraft, MC Flame offers something for everyone — from casual building to intense PvP combat and ranked leaderboards.

Server Rules

Follow these rules to keep MC Flame safe, fair, and fun for everyone.

📜

General Rules

  • Respect all players and staff members.
  • No bullying, toxicity, hate speech, or harassment.
  • Keep chat clean and friendly.
  • Do not impersonate staff or other players.
🛡️

Gameplay Rules

  • No hacking, cheating, auto-clickers, or unfair mods.
  • No exploiting bugs or glitches.
  • No griefing or stealing in protected areas.
  • Play fairly and use common sense.
💬

Chat Rules

  • No spam, flood, or repeated messages.
  • No advertising other servers or websites.
  • No scams, fake giveaways, or misleading links.
  • Use English or Hindi politely in public chat.
⚔️

PvP Rules

  • No combat logging during fights.
  • No trap abuse or unfair spawn killing.
  • No teaming with hackers or rule breakers.
  • Respect safe zones and server boundaries.
👑

Staff Rules

  • Listen to staff instructions.
  • Do not argue during punishments; appeal calmly.
  • Report rule breakers with proof when possible.
  • Staff decisions are final in urgent situations.
🔥

MC Flame Rule

  • Do not ruin the experience for other players.
  • Be honest, fair, and helpful.
  • Protect the community vibe.
  • Have fun and enjoy the server.
Breaking rules can lead to warnings, mute, kick, temporary ban, or permanent ban depending on the situation.
